An AI-Powered Intelligence Platform for Infrastructure Delivery

Project Orient is a data-driven asset intelligence platform that captures lessons from past projects and applies them to future delivery. It will be our organisation's central nervous system for all infrastructure projects, turning our vast archive of documents into a dynamic, queryable knowledge base.

  • 1. Automated Data Extraction

    Using Natural Language Processing (NLP) to read and structure insights from PDFs, reports, and legacy files.

  • 2. Centralised, Searchable Repository

    A unified knowledge base to instantly find data on past projects, materials, and costs.

  • 3. Predictive Analytics

    Applying machine learning to forecast material needs and flag planning risks.

  • 4. Automated Compliance & Sustainability Reporting

    Automatically generate regulatory reports, reducing manual effort.

  • 5. Continuous Improvement Engine

    A system that "learns" from every new project, capturing lessons and refining its recommendations.

Significant Cost Savings

Optimise planning, improve procurement, and prevent costly project errors.

Proactive Risk Mitigation

Use predictive analytics to spot bottlenecks and compliance issues before they occur.

Enhanced Sustainability

Enable data-driven decisions and automate sustainability reporting.

Future Revenue Generation

Create a platform with the potential to be licensed to other infrastructure operators.
